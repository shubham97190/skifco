Who We Are

A trust for community welfare, practical support, and sustainable change

SKIFCO Foundation has been formed to serve the general public with special attention to underserved, rural, and marginalized communities. Our work is built around practical pathways to learning, healthcare access, skill development, environment care, and social inclusion.

We believe real impact happens when support is connected. A child learns better with nutrition and care, a young person grows faster with skills and mentorship, and a community becomes stronger when dignity is protected alongside opportunity.

What We Do

Our Focus Areas

Creating impact across key areas of public welfare through community-focused programs, partnerships, and long-term initiatives.

Education

Schools, scholarships, digital classrooms, literacy programs for children and youth

Healthcare

Health camps, mobile medical units, nutrition, maternal and child welfare support

Skill Development

IT skills, vocational training, entrepreneurship, women empowerment programs

Environment

Tree plantation, water conservation, waste management, clean technology awareness

Innovation & STEM

Robotics, AI learning, science labs, coding clubs, innovation challenges

Self-help groups, women & child welfare, cultural preservation, social justice

Disaster Relief

Elderly Care

Senior citizen support, health check-ups, dignity and livelihood assistance

Why This Work Matters

Connected Challenges Need Connected Solutions

Many families face multiple challenges at the same time. A child may need education support, a parent may need livelihood training, an elderly person may need healthcare, and the community may need clean water, sanitation, and awareness.

Our Trust aims to respond to these needs through connected and responsible social programs.

Education Access

Support first-generation learners, reduce school dropout

Healthcare Access

Preventive healthcare and early diagnosis in rural areas

Livelihood Support

Skills training for youth and women entrepreneurship

Sustainable Future

Environmental responsibility and climate-resilient communities
